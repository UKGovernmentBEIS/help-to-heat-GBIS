Is your loft fully insulated?

A loft is fully insulated if it has more than 270mm (about 10-11 inches) of insulation covering the whole loft, usually laid on the "floor" of the loft with one layer between the horizontal timbers and another layer going across them to bring the insulation up to the required level.

Help me answer this question

If your home was built after 2002, or you have installed loft insulation since 2002, it is likely that it is fully insulated.
